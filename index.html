ABOUT ME

Welcome! My name is Rosemary. I am currently a sophomore in Excelsior Academy, a New York State P-TECH school designed to give students the opportunity to graduate with a high school diploma and earn an Associate’s in Applied Science Degree in Networking or Cyber Security. I plan to get mine in Cyber Security and graduate in four years, so that I can go into the workforce and become independent. I currently have some experience with coding, as I created this website. Since the seventh grade I have been a part of the National Junior Honor Society. It has only made me focused on doing my best to be successful. I took a Watson Analytics Course over the summer of 2017 and was chosen to participate in General Assembly’s ten week HTML, CSS & Web Design Circuit, where I received a certificate. Currently, one of my interests is Graphic Design and I am very comfortable using Photoshop. I enjoy being able to start off with something small and expand and come up with something extraordinary, I almost feel powerful! These experiences have so far expanded my knowledge thus I feel that with more effort in the time to come, I will be able to accomplish even more./p>
